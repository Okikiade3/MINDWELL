Our Services

Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA) for Children

Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA) is an evidence-based intervention designed to improve socially significant behaviours. At Mindwell Psychological & Wellness Services, we provide individualized ABA programs for children, particularly those with autism spectrum disorder and other developmental challenges.

ABA services include:

  • Behavioural assessment and intervention planning
  • Functional Behaviour Assessment (FBA)
  • Skill acquisition programs (communication, social, self-help skills)
  • Behaviour reduction strategies
  • Parent training and caregiver support
  • School consultation and collaboration

Psychoeducational Assessment

Our psychoeducational assessments help identify learning strengths, difficulties, and educational needs in children and adolescents.

This service assesses:

  • Cognitive functioning
  • Academic skills
  • Attention and executive functioning
  • Learning difficulties
  • Behavioural and emotional factors affecting learning

Outcome: Clear assessment reports with practical recommendations for parents, teachers, and schools.

Clinical Psychological Assessment

We conduct comprehensive clinical assessments to understand emotional, behavioural, and psychological functioning.

Assessments may include:

  • Personality assessment
  • Emotional and behavioural evaluation
  • Diagnostic clarification
  • Mental health screening

Purpose:To guide diagnosis, treatment planning, and intervention decisions.

Psychotherapy

Our psychotherapy services provide a safe, confidential space for individuals to explore emotional challenges and develop coping strategies.

We offer therapy for:

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Stress-related difficulties
  • Adjustment challenges
  • Behavioural concerns
  • Emotional regulation difficulties

Approach:Evidence-based therapeutic techniques tailored to individual needs.

Virtual Therapy

We provide professional psychological services through secure online platforms, making therapy accessible regardless of location.

Virtual therapy is suitable for:

  • Adults and adolescents
  • Follow-up therapy sessions
  • Clients who prefer remote support

Benefits:

  • Convenience
  • Privacy
  • Flexibility
