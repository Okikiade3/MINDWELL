Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do you offer services for children with autism?

Yes. We provide Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA) and related behavioural interventions.

Are your services confidential?

Yes. All services are conducted with strict confidentiality and ethical standards.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes. Virtual therapy sessions are available.

Do you work with schools?

Yes. We collaborate with schools for assessments, interventions, and consultations.
